May peace & blessings be upon you! MantledInModesty is brought to you by four Pakistani sisters, born and raised in Chicago, IL. We strive to provide high quality, stylish and colorful scarves to women everywhere. We aspire to support the expression of modesty in America at a time where doing so can seem intimidating. Finding hues and fabrics to match your every occasion and daily wear can be also quite a challenge. These reasons are precisely why we launched MantledInModesty. ✨ Elegance shouldn’t have to make you file for bankruptcy!!
